Where should Fatigue, Corruption & Insanity live?

You said "move them elsewhere" — here are three homes. (Shown above: Option A.)

A

Persistent status rail under the header

Always visible on every tab as slim bars. Best for at-a-glance tracking; costs a sliver of vertical space.

B

Compact chips in the header (next to Fate)

Three small clickable counters beside Fate. Saves space, keeps all "character resources" together; less room for bars.

C

On the Combat sub-tab with Wounds
